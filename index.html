arrow_circle_left The Beginning


I came to UCF thinking I would be a game developer. I created some games and pushed my artwork further than I ever had before. As I learned more about the industry, I realized that I wanted to be a developer of many things, and not restricted to just games. I began to explore my artwork and left the idea of game development behind.

arrow_circle_right The Future


I really fell in love with programming, even more than I did with art. I switched my track to web design as the classes make more sense. However, my true goals lie far beyond a degree. I want to develop software thats exciting. I will have my own company and also create collectibles. I will always use both my artistic talents to design and my coding skills to create.
